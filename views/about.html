This was when I first discovered my affinity for working with technology and web development. Since my time in Thailand I maintained an interest in web development, until finally deciding to enroll at Grand Circus Developer Bootcamp in Detroit. I completed their Front-End Development program on March 24th with a great understanding and implementation of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and more. Since my first day at Grand Circus I have significantly molded and cultivated my passion for web development. I am able to learn in a fast-paced environment, create whatever I can imagine, and implement relevant industry material on a daily basis.
